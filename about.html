About CrimeTimeSnacks

A true crime podcast that delves into unsolved cases, exploring the facts, theories, and evidence surrounding each mystery.

Our Mission

CrimeTimeSnacks is a true crime podcast that delves into a variety of unsolved cases, exploring the facts, theories, and evidence surrounding each mystery. The podcast aims to provide listeners with a comprehensive understanding of these cases, encouraging them to engage with the content and form their own conclusions.

Each episode is meticulously researched to bring you the most accurate information about some of the most intriguing criminal cases in history. We believe in presenting the facts without sensationalism, respecting the victims and their families while still delivering compelling storytelling.

Whether you're a true crime enthusiast or new to the genre, CrimeTimeSnacks offers accessible, informative, and thought-provoking content that will keep you coming back for more.

Meet Your Host

Cory

Cory is the creator and host of CrimeTimeSnacks. With a background in research and a lifelong fascination with true crime, Cory brings a unique perspective to each case covered on the podcast.

What started as a personal interest in unsolved mysteries quickly evolved into a passion for sharing these stories with others. Cory spends countless hours researching each case, digging through court documents, news reports, and interviews to provide listeners with the most comprehensive coverage possible.

When not recording episodes, Cory can be found reading true crime books, watching documentaries, and engaging with the CrimeTimeSnacks community online.
