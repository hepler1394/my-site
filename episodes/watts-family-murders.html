The Watts Family Murders

March 16, 2025

In this episode of Crime Time Snacks, we uncover the shocking case of the Watts Family Murders, a story of betrayal and deception that horrified the nation in 2018.

On August 13, 2018, Shanann Watts, a 34-year-old pregnant mother, along with her two young daughters, Bella, 4, and Celeste, 3, were reported missing in Frederick, Colorado. The case took a horrifying turn when her husband, Chris Watts, initially pleaded for their safe return but was later arrested for their murders.

Chris Watts later confessed to strangling his wife and suffocating his two daughters before disposing of their bodies in oil tanks at his workplace. The chilling details of the case and his lack of remorse shocked the public, leading to a media frenzy and an in-depth investigation.

In this episode, we break down the timeline of events, analyze Watts' disturbing confessions, and discuss the role of forensic evidence and psychological profiling in bringing him to justice.
