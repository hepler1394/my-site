The Delphi Murder Case

March 16, 2025

In this episode of Crime Time Snacks, we investigate the heartbreaking and mysterious Delphi Murders.

On February 13, 2017, 13-year-old Abigail Williams and 14-year-old Liberty German vanished while hiking on the Monon High Bridge in Delphi, Indiana. Their bodies were discovered the next day, and the case has since puzzled investigators.

Liberty German managed to capture crucial evidence on her phone, including an image and a voice recording of the suspected killer saying, "Down the hill." Despite this, the perpetrator remains unidentified.

In this episode, we examine the evidence, theories, and latest updates in one of America's most haunting unsolved cases.
