Suggest a Case

Do you have a case you'd like us to cover on CrimeTimeSnacks? We're always looking for interesting and compelling stories to share with our listeners.

Case Submission Guidelines

  • Provide as much detail about the case as possible
  • Include links to reputable sources if available
  • Let us know why you think this case would be interesting for our listeners
  • Mention if you have a personal connection to the case

You can submit case suggestions using the contact form above or by emailing us directly at cases@crimetimesnack.com

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 3-5 business days. Due to the volume of messages we receive, it may sometimes take longer, but we do read every message.

Can I request an interview or collaboration?

Yes! We're open to interviews and collaborations with other podcasters, content creators, and experts in relevant fields. Please use the contact form and select "Business/Sponsorship" as the subject.

Do you accept sponsorships or advertising?

Yes, we are open to sponsorships and advertising opportunities that align with our podcast's values and would be of interest to our listeners. Please contact us for more information.
